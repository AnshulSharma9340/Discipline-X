How to use

On your website: drop icon.svg in your assets and reference it directly. For the favicon, add <link rel="icon" type="image/svg+xml" href="favicon.svg"> to your <head>.

For app stores / Instagram profile: open icon.svg in any browser, then use a free converter like cloudconvert.com to export a 1024×1024 PNG. Or open in Figma / Illustrator → export at any size.
